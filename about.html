Our Mission

"A one-stop center for business enterprise that does the right thing for our clients, our people, and our communities at large." To be a center of excellence and best practice in offering demand-driven, results-oriented management solutions to institutions in the public and private sectors as well as Civil Society organizations in Rwanda and throughout the region. In order to be the greatest in Rwanda and the surrounding area, we want to create recognizable professional services that are constantly in the forefront of people's minds. We lead the professional agenda and establish the benchmark.

Our Vision

Our strong commitment to producing high-quality work, meeting deadlines, providing efficient services, maintaining integrity, accepting professional responsibility, and guaranteeing value for money in accordance with our clients' interests is what propels our corporate philosophy, daily operations, and success.

Organization

Proven Consultants Ltd., a development, management, and research consulting organization, was founded and incorporated in Rwanda. In order to create a network of highly skilled and competitive world-class professionals that offer development and management solutions that are driven by demand and results, the firm brings together professionals and practitioners in a variety of disciplines from Rwanda, other countries in the region, and international sources. The primary areas of technical proficiency and professional specialization are:

  • Institutional & organizational development
  • Research
  • Business advisory services
  • Accounting services
  • Post harvest operational activities
  • Human Resource services
